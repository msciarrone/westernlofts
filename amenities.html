Amenities

The amenities at St Paul and Western Lofts are designed for everyone. Each unit features sleek modern finishes, hardwood floors, central heating and air conditioning, and washer/dryer in-unit. Select units can enjoy their own private balcony. Residents can enjoy scenic views of the city from our rooftop deck. Take advantage of our community bike racks, gym, and park your car safely in our covered garage.
